Six capabilities — each solving a real problem that contractors, government agencies, and project managers face when procuring electrical goods for serious projects.

    Project Supply — Wholesale

    We supply authorised LED luminaires, lamps, fans, high mast poles, and electrical accessories at the volumes and lead times that serious infrastructure projects demand. Every product comes from OEM-authorised stock, carrying full manufacturer warranty and compliance certification. Whether you are procuring for a single building or a national highway project, our supply capability scales to your requirement.

    GeM & Government Tenders

Ltd. is an active GeM seller (Seller ID: 049D180000460368), supplying BIS-certified products to government agencies, PSUs, and central and state government departments.     GeM · Government Full Details
  • 03

    SITC — Turnkey Solutions

    For clients who require a single point of accountability from product supply through to operational handover, we offer full SITC services managed through vetted execution partners. One purchase order, one contract, one warranty — covering supply of all specified luminaires, professional installation, commissioning testing, and sign-off. Available for commercial, industrial, and outdoor lighting projects.

    Supply · Install · Test · Commission Enquire
  • 04

    Specification Translation

    When a tender specification names a discontinued product, a brand for which you lack an authorised distributor, or a competitor-specific model that cannot be sourced at the required price or timeline — we translate it. We identify the currently available OEM-compliant equivalent, confirm its technical parity, and document the substitution for the procurement record. A service that saves procurement teams significant time and avoids specification disputes on site.

    Tender Spec · Substitution Enquire
  • 05

    Kitting & Bundling

    A project lighting order is rarely just luminaires. It includes drivers, mounting accessories, cabling accessories, control gear, and installation hardware. We assemble complete, project-ready kits — primary products plus every accessory required for installation — dispatched under one purchase order, one invoice, and one delivery. Fewer suppliers, fewer POs, fewer delays on site.

    Single PO · Single Invoice Enquire
  • 06

    Pan-India Supply & Dispatch

    Our supply capability is not limited to Delhi NCR. We have dispatched to project sites in Jammu, Maharashtra, and across India — managing e-way bill documentation, freight coordination, and site delivery logistics for outstation projects. If your project is outside Delhi, ask us. We have done it before.

    E-Way Bill · Freight · Logistics Enquire
