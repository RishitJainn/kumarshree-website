Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how kumarshree.com, operated jointly by M.S. Kumar Electricals Private Limited (GST: 07AABCM0298G1ZJ) and Kumar Shree Electricals (GST: 07ABAFK4763B1ZM), collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information submitted through this website.

By using this website or submitting an enquiry form, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information through this website:

Contact & Enquiry Forms: Name, company name, designation, telephone number, and email address submitted via website contact forms, tender upload forms, or catalogue download forms.

Usage Data: Anonymous data collected via Google Analytics, including pages visited, time on site, device type, and geographic location (city/region level). This data does not identify you personally.

Communications: Records of enquiries submitted through the website, including the content of messages and requirement descriptions.

3

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through this website is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry, quotation request, or tender specification submission
  • To send you product information, pricing, or compliance documents you have requested
  • To follow up on an active commercial discussion or project inquiry
  • To send catalogue downloads you have requested via the Catalogues page
  • To improve the website experience and content based on anonymous usage analytics
We do not use your contact details for unsolicited marketing campaigns. We do not sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to third parties.
4

Catalogue Downloads — Lead Capture

When you request a product catalogue download on our Catalogues page, you are required to provide your name and either a phone number or email address before the download begins. This information is used solely to follow up with relevant product information or pricing.

You may opt out of further contact at any time by emailing lighting@kumarshree.com.

5

Tender & Specification Uploads

Documents uploaded via the Tender Specification Upload form on our GeM & Government Supply page are shared only with our internal project and procurement team.

These documents are treated as confidential commercial information and are not shared with third parties.
6

Cookies

This website uses cookies to support basic functionality and to enable Google Analytics. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some website functionality may be affected.

7

Data Retention

Contact and enquiry data is retained for a period of three years from the date of submission, in line with standard commercial record-keeping practice.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at lighting@kumarshree.com.

8

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information submitted through this website against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. This includes secure hosting on password-protected servers.

However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to any legal retention obligations
  • Withdraw consent for further contact at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: lighting@kumarshree.com or sandeep.kumarelec@gmail.com

10

Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party platforms including Google Drive (for catalogue downloads) and gem.gov.in. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and recommend you review their respective privacy policies.

11

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. The most current version will always be available at www.kumarshree.com/privacy-policy.html.

Material changes will be noted by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
