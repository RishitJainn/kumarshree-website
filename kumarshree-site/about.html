Our Story

Four decades of project-specialist supply

Since 1985, the team behind kumarshree.com has been supplying India's most demanding infrastructure projects with the lighting products they require. What began as a single electrical trade operation at Ajmeri Gate, Old Delhi, has grown into two complementary entities — M.S. Kumar Electricals Pvt. Ltd. and Kumar Shree Electricals — each serving distinct markets under the same philosophy: authorised products, expert guidance, and project-specialist supply.

Over four decades, we have earned formal authorisations from Bajaj, Philips, Havells, Crompton, Halonix, and Orient Electric, and delivered for clients ranging from AIIMS Jammu and Delhi Metro to Signature Bridge and NHAI expressways. Our clients are contractors, government agencies, PSUs, defence establishments, and real estate developers who need a distributor that understands what project supply actually demands.

We do not sell from a catalogue. We solve procurement problems.

Entity 1 · Private Limited Company · Est. 1985

M.S. Kumar Electricals
Private Limited

Since 1985 GeM Registered ★ Bajaj Top Distributor — North India

M.S. Kumar Electricals Private Limited has been operating since 1985 and was formally incorporated in 1994. Headquartered at Ajmeri Gate, New Delhi, it is one of India's leading authorised distributors for LED luminaires, lamps, fans, high mast poles, and electrical accessories. Recognised as a leading distributor in North India for Bajaj Electricals, MSKEPL holds an active GeM seller registration and has delivered project supply for government hospitals, metro rail stations, international airports, defence establishments, and national highway infrastructure across India.
